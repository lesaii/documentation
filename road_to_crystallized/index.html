What is SN0W ＜＞＜ CRYSTALL1ZED?

CRYSTALL1ZED ＜＞＜ is a content creation community that is an addition to ROSE ＜＞＜. It serves both as a tutorial for ROSE ＜＞＜ (CRYSTALL1ZED I), as well being a sort of DLC after you have joined ROSE ＜＞＜(CRYSTALL1ZED II, CRYSTALL1ZED III, CRYSTALL1ZED IV, and CRYSTALL1ZED V)

When someone gets CRYSTALL1ZED they get exclusive access to things like:

  • Participate in votes in upcoming Crystallizations!
  • You can share 3D models, World Prefabs, Avatar Prefabs, Art and Graphics, Videos and Films, Sound Effects and other stuff with everyone on this server
  • Access to the exclusive discord channel made for CRYSTALL1ZED ＜＞＜ members
  • CRYSTALL1ZED Role
  • Invitation to the CRYSTALL1ZED ＜＞＜ Group

 

How do I become CRYSTALL1ZED?

To become CRYSTALL1ZED you need to make a contribution in The SN0W ＜＞＜ Discord Server that requires human effort to create. For example:

  • Public VRChat avatar/world
  • Films
  • 3D models
  • Script, Code, Utility, Addon, Tools
  • Shaders
  • Videos/Animations
  • Music, Sound Effects only youtube or google drive links allowed
  • Drawings
  • and more! As long as it's related to ＜＞＜ and requires effort to create.

Make your contribution concise and presentable. A good presentation, especially a video, is the best way to win votes.

Contributions that contain NSFW content are not allowed.

You can submit a contribution every 6 hours

Is there a difference between contributions for CRYSTALL1ZAT10N and contributions for ROSE ＜＞＜?

No, there isn't. both contributions are contributions for the ＜＞＜ community as a whole, so go ham with it!

 

There's levels to this?

yes, CRYSTALL1ZED ＜＞＜ has levels, ranging from 1 to 5

  • CRYSTALL1ZED I means that you have successfully contributed 1 creation for Crystallization

  • CRYSTALL1ZED II means that you contributed 1 creation that took a significant amount of effort to pull off, for Crystallization

  • CRYSTALL1ZED III means that you contributed a creation that has inspired 2 people to create something with definitive proof

  • CRYSTALL1ZED IV means that you have inspired 10 people to pass Crystallization II with definitive proof

  • CRYSTALL1ZED V means that you have contributed 10 CRYSTALLIZED II level creations that have successfully inspired 50 people to pass CRYSTALLIZED II themselves with definitive proof

How will votes be decided?

After submitting a contribution it will be reviewed by le Saii and decided If a vote is necessary, if so all the CRYSTALL1ZED members will participate in a 2-day long voting process. To receive a CRYSTALL1ZED ＜＞＜ invitation‚ the contribution must receive a YES vote from over 60％ of the voting CRYSTALL1ZED members․

But the criteria on which the vote will be decided upon depends on which level you're going for

For CRYSTALL1ZED I, 4 points will be considered for you to be accepted

  • Fun: Is it fun?
  • Presentability: Is the contribution well presented and easy to understand?
  • Effort: How much human effort was needed to make this? How much did AI help?
  • Collaboration: How many people were involved in creating the contribution?

For CRYSTALL1ZED II, 6 points will be considered for you to be accepted(same criteria as ROSE ＜＞＜)

  • Fun: Is it fun?
  • Presentability: Is the contribution well presented and easy to understand?
  • Effort: How much human effort was needed to make this? How much did AI help?
  • Usability: Can it be used for something?
  • Originality: How different is the contribution from previous contributions?
  • Collaboration: How many people were involved in creating the contribution?

For CRYSTALL1ZED III, only 2 points will be considered

  • Inspiration: Has your creation inspired 2 people?
  • Creation: Have those who were inspired by you, created anything?

For CRYSTALL1ZED IV, only 2 points will be considered as well, but on a much higher level

  • Inspiration Has your creation inspired 10 people?
  • Creation Have those 10 people made and contributed enough to be CRYSTALL1ZED II?

CRYSTALL1ZED V, is split into 2 requirements, and those have separate criteria

Requirement 1:

Make 10 creations that satisfy CRYSTALL1ZED II requirements

Requirement 2:

Those 10 creations have successfully made 50 people make and contribute enough to be CRYSTALL1ZED II?
