OFFICIAL NOTES

ROSE ＜＞＜ is a content creation community that makes content for the ＜＞＜ community․

When someone joins ROSE ＜＞＜, they get access to exclusive things like:

  • Participate in votes for upcoming ROSE ＜＞＜ members at a dedicated ROSE ＜＞＜ submission channel for future ROSE ＜＞＜ members
  • Name placement on the SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS boards in VRChat worlds
  • Your projects will appear in the ROSE ＜＞＜ database
  • You can share 3D models, Art Graphics, Sound effects and other stuff with everyone on this server.
  • Avatars more promoted at the ＜＞＜ AVATARS world
  • Access to the exclusive discord channels made for ROSE ＜＞＜ members
  • An exclusive ROSE ＜＞＜ discord role
  • Invitation to the ROSE ＜＞＜ VRChat group

QUALIFICATIONS

To join ROSE ＜＞＜ you need to make a contribution that requires human effort to create and could be easily used by the fish community members. For example:

  • Public VRChat avatar/world
  • Films
  • 3D models
  • Script, Code, Utility, Addon, Tools
  • Shaders
  • Videos/Animations
  • Music, Sound Effects only youtube or google drive links allowed
  • Drawings
  • and more! As long as it's related to ＜＞＜ and requires effort to create.

Make your contribution concise and presentable. A good presentation, especially a video, is the best way to win votes.

Contributions that contain NSFW content are not allowed.

 

After submitting a contribution it will be reviewed by TheZiver and decided If a vote is necessary, if so all the rose fish members will participate in a 2-day long voting process. To receive a rose fish invitation‚ the contribution must receive a YES vote from over 60％ of the voting rose fish members․

Deciding if a vote is necessary TheZiver will consider these things:

  • Fun: Is it fun?
  • Presentability: Is the contribution well presented and easy to understand?
  • Effort: How much human effort was needed to make this? How much did AI help?
  • Usability: Can it be used for something?
  • Originality: How different is the contribution from previous contributions?
  • Collaboration: How many people were involved in creating the contribution?
